Facebook

Facebook is a social networking platform launched in 2004 that allows users to create profiles, share updates, photos, and videos, connect with friends and family, join groups, and follow pages of interest. It is widely used for personal interaction, community building, and business marketing.

Instagram

Instagram is a photo and video sharing app introduced in 2010, known for its visual content and storytelling features. Users can post images and short videos, use filters, share Stories (which disappear after 24 hours), and explore content through hashtags. It is popular for its emphasis on aesthetics and influencer culture.

Twitter

Twitter, launched in 2006, is a microblogging platform where users post short messages called "tweets" (up to 280 characters). It is used for real-time updates, news dissemination, and public conversations. Hashtags and trending topics help users follow and participate in broader discussions.
